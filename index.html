Storage instructions

The crispy chips are designed to be stored for a long time when the customer follows the storage instructions carefully.

  • 1. Always keep the chips in the refregrator with temperaure below ZERO degrees
  • 2. Always keep the chips covered with no access of moisture from outside for long term storage
  • 3. Make sure that you read the expiry date before using the product.

Preparation for best results

When prepared in the right way, our products give the best test.

  • 1. Boil the cooking oil in the pan upto 170 degrees
  • 2. Deep fry the product for 8-10 minutes depending on the cruchy level inteaded
  • 3. For best results, always fry the frozen product.
